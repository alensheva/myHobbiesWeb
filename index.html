Welcome To My World!

A hobby is considered to be a regular activity that is done for enjoyment, typically during one's leisure time.

Probably, each of us has his own hobby - an occupation for the soul, a favorite activity. We devote our free time to hobbies, discuss our hobbies, brag about our achievements..

So, what does the word "hobby" mean?
In Old England, farmers loved their horses very much and decorated them with flowers. Robbin, Dobbin and Hobbin were the most popular names. The name Hobbin changed to Hobby - that's how people called toy horses, with which they loved to play and collect. And only after a while, the word hobby got its modern meaning - "what you like to play, do in your free time."

Any occupation can become a hobby: sports, modeling, collecting, needlework, growing plants, your own pet corner and many, many other activities.
